Chairman

What we need to do is to regain the original purity of our hearts and become truly human. Only then can we die happily as humans with clarity. What I am showing you is the way up there. Quthbuzzaman has shown us the path to elevate those who approach with sincere intention, by nurturing the divine qualities within, and lead them to the lofty state of being God's most excellent creation. That is what I continue to do. It is the method of spiritual purification that will continue until the Day of Judgment. Quthbuzzaman taught us to look at the heart of a person, regardless of caste or creed. It is when one attains that blessed state of mind that one achieves complete success in life. That is what I guarantee.


— Nāeb Quthbuzzāman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shāh Qādiri Chishti

Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti is a Sufi master, socio-religious reformer, and a true champion of communal harmony, futuristic education, humanitarian healthcare, and philanthropic support. Being the current leading master of Qadiriyyah Sufi path of peace and love and the custodian of ‘Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef - Kerala’, the global centre of peace and humanity, Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti is the successor of the renowned modern Indian Sufi master and scholar Hazrat / His Holiness Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Sufi Muhammad Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, the reviver of Sufism in modern Kerala and the founder of ‘Jeelani Trust’. With the evidently increased impetus and enormous growth and impact, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan is taking forward Hazrat Quthbuzzaman’s multifaceted undertakings for rebuilding an ethical humanity and progressive unity among social and religious communities.

Under the functional platform of Jeelani Trust and beyond, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan carries out various missions, to cater the educational, health, economic, and spiritual needs and aspirations of the people within the region and outside. Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan is meticulously leading a devout and massive mission to bring about inspirational hope and eventual happiness to the humanity at large, widely opening the Sufi path of peace and love, practically and philosophically based on Islamic traditions and humanist outlook. He is the frontrunner of the ‘Jeelani’ movement of social reform and Sufi guidance, ‘Ghausiyya’ council of Islamic scholars, ‘Nizamia’ ventures of Islamic and liberal education, and ‘Thaqdees’ enterprise for the healthcare and wellness. He has been also compassionate in leading several humanitarian charity missions across diverse social sectors, including education, health, public safety, etc. To the peace-seekers from various social and religious backgrounds who visit Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan keenly attends with moral guidance and spiritual blessings.

Being a socially committed Islamic leader with universal orientation, the Sufi social mission of Naeb Quthbuzzaman graciously represents two important themes of current global salience: social harmony and world peace. The predominant mission he undertakes, however, is that of a compassionate Sufi, set out to realize an ethically empowered and spiritually enlightened humanity. All his teachings and initiatives clearly emphasize that the noble task he accomplishes for all kinds of humans who reach out to him is careful guidance for ethical self-becoming. His public efforts to promote peace and harmony were also recognized by the Honorary Doctorate in ‘Human Rights & Peace’ (2021) conferred to him by the UN-accredited Globe Eagle University, authorized by the government of California, USA.

In the sacred list (‘silsila’) of Qadiri Sufi masters, following Hzt. Quthbuzzaman’s Sufi path of spiritual perfection in Tawhid represented by the holy Prophetic examples, Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Nizamuddin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti emerged as the 36th descendant of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), and was chosen as the 19th Sufi successor of Ghaus al-A’zam Shaikh Muhyiddin Abdul Qadir al-Jeelani (may he be blessed), the supreme master and the founder of Qadiri Sufi order at Baghdad. Taking up the phenomenal mission of reviving the original and humane spirit of din (religion), Naeb Quthbuzzaman also insists on returning to the more amicable path of traditional Islam the foundation of which is tauhid (divine unity) and thasawwuf (spiritual purity/Sufism). Unlike the exclusionary ideologies of the politically motivated fundamentalists and modern extremists, Naeb Quthbuzzaman treads in the virtuous footsteps of Hzt. Quthbuzzaman, seeking to revive the elemental Sufi vitality of Islamic tradition for its clarity of the concept of humanity and its practically sound methods of spiritual re-unification of human societies.

Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan was born on 5th May 1975, at Aluva (Ernakulam), Kerala. He belongs to the scholarly household of ‘Vennipparambu’ which emerged in west-central Kerala centuries ago out of the Indo-Arab transregional connections in religion, commerce and culture, and rooted in the cosmopolitan currents of Cochin. Presently, it is Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan who leads the regional cosmopolitan heritage of Sufism, by re-presenting Islam for humanity’s survival as ethically mindful and morally interconnected subjects, and with the pluralist precepts to engage human and non-human diversity on the planet earth.

The projects and programme laid under Jeelani Trust to realize the Sufi Islamic, social and humanitarian mission are being successfully carried forward to much advanced levels by the astute successor chosen by Hzt. Dr. Quthbuzzaman. Against all odds, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah is relentless in leading the social and moral mission of Hzt. Quthbuzzaman, by caring for the poor and mobilizing the masses to the horizon of piety and progress. Youthful and imaginative, he stands as the moral beacon of hope for the spiritually and culturally aspiring humanity. He keeps devising social and educational strategies to ethically equip the youth to face the impending challenges of the times. A humane role model, Naeb Quthbuzzaman performs the leadership for the young generation of citizens around the world, educating the youth in moral integrity and empowering them to selflessly serve humanity; what is understood to be the pressing need of the perilous present that is riven by moral cynicism, natural calamities and pandemics. As the Sufi leadership of the present, Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan confidently re-presents the ethical traditions laid out by the Holy Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) and propagated by Hzt. Quthbuzzaman, for the late-modern times of human and planetary precarities.

Naeb Quthbuzzaman has dedicated his life to the multipronged Jeelani-Sufi mission, in order to rekindle the sublime message of harmony between persons, places and nations, in the current world of inter-cultural distrust, religious and racial hatred, and the problems of terrorism and violence. Leading a pious humanitarian mission of Nizamia Futures for realizing a sober Sufi humanity of peace and progress, Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan’s promise is the ethical transformation of human subjectivities across the cultures around the world.
